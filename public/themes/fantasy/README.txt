FANTASY THEME ASSETS
====================

Place your fantasy-themed assets in this folder:

IMAGES (PNG with transparency recommended):
- parchment.png     - Dialog box background (800x200px)
- button.png        - Button background (200x50px)
- border.png        - Decorative border (tileable)

SOUNDS (MP3 or WAV):
- click.mp3         - Button click (paper/parchment sound)
- transition.mp3    - Scene transition (magical whoosh)
- hover.mp3         - Button hover (subtle rustle)

RECOMMENDED STYLE:
- Warm colors (browns, golds, burgundy)
- Parchment/paper textures
- Medieval/fantasy aesthetic
- Serif fonts (Cinzel, Merriweather)
