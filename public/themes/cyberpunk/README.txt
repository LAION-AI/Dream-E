CYBERPUNK THEME ASSETS
======================

Place your cyberpunk-themed assets in this folder:

IMAGES (PNG with transparency recommended):
- panel.png         - Dialog box background (800x200px)
- button.png        - Button background (200x50px)
- circuit.png       - Circuit pattern overlay (tileable)

SOUNDS (MP3 or WAV):
- beep.mp3          - Button click (digital beep)
- glitch.mp3        - Scene transition (glitchy)
- hover.mp3         - Button hover (electronic hum)

RECOMMENDED STYLE:
- Dark backgrounds (near black)
- Neon accent colors (cyan, magenta, pink)
- Sharp edges (no rounded corners)
- Monospace/digital fonts (Orbitron, Roboto Mono)
- Glow effects (CSS handles this)
